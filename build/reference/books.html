Recommended Books

By Skill Level​

Beginner (Under 1200)​

BookAuthorNotes
Silman's Complete Endgame CourseJeremy SilmanRating-based progression, excellent for beginners

Intermediate (1200-1800)​

BookAuthorNotes
100 Endgames You Must KnowJesus de la VillaPractical, essential positions
Endgame StrategyMikhail ShereshevskyTheme-based, teaches strategic thinking

Advanced (1800+)​

BookAuthorNotes
Dvoretsky's Endgame ManualMark DvoretskyComprehensive, master-level
Fundamental Chess EndingsMüller & LamprechtEncyclopedia, excellent reference

Specialized​

BookAuthorTopic
Rook EndingsLevenfish & SmyslovDefinitive rook endgame reference
Secrets of Pawn EndingsMüller & LamprechtDeep pawn endgame coverage

How to Choose​

  1. Start with Silman if you're under 1600
  2. Add de la Villa when you reach 1600
  3. Use Dvoretsky as a reference when you reach 2000
  4. Specialize with topical books as needed

  • Lichess Practice — Free endgame drills
  • Chessable — Spaced repetition courses
  • Syzygy Tablebases — Perfect play lookup
