# Intermediate Path (1200-1600)

Build on your fundamentals with standard theoretical positions that every club player must know.

**Prerequisites:** [Beginner Path](/study-paths/beginner) — basic checkmates, opposition, K+P vs K.

## Learning Path

1. **[Lucena position](/rook-endgames/lucena-position)**
   The winning technique for rook + pawn vs rook when your pawn is on the 7th rank — build the bridge and promote.

2. **[Philidor position](/rook-endgames/philidor-position)**
   The drawing technique for defending rook + pawn vs rook — keep the rook on the third rank.

3. **[Rook vs pawn](/rook-endgames/rook-vs-pawn)**
   When one side has just a rook against a single pawn — learn when the rook can stop it and when it cannot.

4. **[Triangulation](/pawn-endgames/triangulation)**
   Lose a tempo by moving your king in a triangle, transferring the move to your opponent and forcing [zugzwang](/strategic-themes/zugzwang).

5. **[Outside passed pawn](/pawn-endgames/outside-passed-pawn)**
   A passed pawn far from the main action — use it as a decoy to divert the enemy king and win on the other side.

6. **[King activity](/fundamentals/king-activity)**
   In the endgame the king is a fighting piece — learn when and how to activate it in the center.
