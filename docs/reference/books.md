# Recommended Books

## By Skill Level

### Beginner (Under 1200)

| Book | Author | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Silman's Complete Endgame Course | Jeremy Silman | Rating-based progression, excellent for beginners |

### Intermediate (1200-1800)

| Book | Author | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| 100 Endgames You Must Know | Jesus de la Villa | Practical, essential positions |
| Endgame Strategy | Mikhail Shereshevsky | Theme-based, teaches strategic thinking |

### Advanced (1800+)

| Book | Author | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Dvoretsky's Endgame Manual | Mark Dvoretsky | Comprehensive, master-level |
| Fundamental Chess Endings | Müller & Lamprecht | Encyclopedia, excellent reference |

### Specialized

| Book | Author | Topic |
|------|--------|-------|
| Rook Endings | Levenfish & Smyslov | Definitive rook endgame reference |
| Secrets of Pawn Endings | Müller & Lamprecht | Deep pawn endgame coverage |

## How to Choose

1. **Start with Silman** if you're under 1600
2. **Add de la Villa** when you reach 1600
3. **Use Dvoretsky** as a reference when you reach 2000
4. **Specialize** with topical books as needed
