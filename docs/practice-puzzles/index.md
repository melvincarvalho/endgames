## How It Works

1. **Drag pieces** to make your move
2. **Green highlight** = correct move
3. **Red highlight** = try again
4. Use **hints** if you get stuck
5. **Reset** to start over

## Why Practice Puzzles?

> "The endgame is like a well-written short story. It has a clear beginning, development, and conclusion."
> — Yuri Averbakh

Puzzles help you:
- **Pattern recognition** — Spot winning positions instantly
- **Calculation** — Think ahead with precision
- **Technique** — Execute flawlessly under pressure
- **Confidence** — Trust your endgame knowledge
